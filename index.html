About the Game

Dream Shepherd is an enchanting puzzle game that takes you on a mystical journey through the realm of dreams. As the Dream Shepherd, your mission is to guide lost dreams back to their rightful owners, solving intricate puzzles and uncovering the secrets of the dream world. With its captivating visuals and immersive gameplay, Dream Shepherd offers a unique experience that will challenge your mind and warm your heart.

How to Play

  1. Left click = Purchase/Place (左键点击 = 购买/放置)
  2. Right click = Operate (右键单击 = 操作)
  3. Use arrow keys or WASD to move the Dream Shepherd
  4. Guide lost dreams to their matching dreamers
  5. Solve puzzles by moving objects and creating paths
  6. Collect special items to unlock new abilities
  7. Complete levels to progress through the dream world
